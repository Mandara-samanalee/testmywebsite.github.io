FACULTY OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty was established in June 2001 to cater to the growing need for Information Technology(IT) professionals in the country. The faculty consists of 03 departments.


  • Department of Information Technology
  • Department of Interdisciplinary Studies
  • Department of Computational Mathematics

Faculty conducts three internal degree programmes, namely,

  1. Bachelor of Science Honours in Information Technology (BScHons (IT))
  2. Bachelor of Science Honours in Information Technology & Management (BScHons (IT&M))
  3. Bachelor of Science Honours in Artificial Intelligence (BScHons (AI))

and the external degree programme, Bachelor of Information Technology.

  • IT Faculty website: http://uom.lk/itfac
